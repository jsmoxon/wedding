Rehearsal Picnic

Friday, August 21, 2020

What

Picnic at The Lafayette Reservoir

You are cordially invited to our rehearsal picnic at the Lafayette Reservoir. We'll be bringing a food truck, beer, wine and soft drinks to a picnic site.

We'll have games and a few short speeches. Mostly, we just want more time to visit with you! Bring a frisbee, soccer ball, whiffle ball. Custom beer by Badger Brewing.

Where

The Lafayette Reservoir - Upper Picnic Site

The Lafayette Reservoir is located at 3849 Mt. Diablo Blvd. in Lafayette, CA 94549. We've reserved the Upper Picnic Site which is to the right of the reservoir as you approach it from the parking lot. It's about a 5 minute walk from the parking lot.

When

Friday, August 21, 2020 from 4pm-sundown

More Info

What else should I know?

We'll have a few sports activities to play on the lawn, but bring anything you would like to play!

There is a ~3 mile paved trail and a ~5 mile dirt trail around the reservoir. Come a bit early if you want to hike it!

Dress casually and cooly! It can be very hot in Lafayette in August.
