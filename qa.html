Q&A

When is the RSVP deadline?

June 15, 2021. Please RSVP via Paperless Post!

Where is it?

The wedding ceremony and reception will take place at The Pleasant Hill Community Center (Perera Pavilion 320 Civic Dr., Pleasant Hill, CA).

Where should I stay?

Depending on traffic, Pleasant Hill is about 45-60 minutes from San Francisco, 30-40 minutes from Oakland/Berkeley, and about 15 minutes from Walnut Creek. There are many options at just about every price point. It's even 45 minutes from Napa if you want to make it a thing!

We haven't stayed at any of these places, but here are a few ideas:

1. Residence Inn Pleasant Hill Concord: .8 mile walk from the wedding venue.

2. Walnut Creek Marriott: 5 miles to venue

3. Residence Inn Walnut Creek: 5.6 miles to venue

4. Holiday Inn Express Walnut Creek: 4.3 miles to venue

5. Embassy Suites Hilton Walnut Creek: 3.6 miles to venue

6. Hyatt House Pleasant Hill: 2.2 miles to venue

7. Lafayette Park Hotel & Spa: 6 miles to venue

Are kids invited?

Please leave your children at home so you can enjoy a night out kiddos-free!

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive before 4:30 as we will be starting the ceremony then!

Where should I park?

There is ample parking at the reception site.

Will food be served?

Yes! We will have appetizers upon arrival at the reception, followed by a taco buffet with plenty of vegan-friendly, gluten-free and non-vegan options!

Is it okay to take pictures during the wedding?

Please refrain from taking photos during the ceremony, but feel free to take photos at any other time!

What should I wear?

Cocktail Attire - fancy & fun, and make sure you bring your dancing shoes too!

Are the ceremony and reception locations wheelchair accessible?

Yes.
