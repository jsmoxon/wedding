Where should I stay?

Lafayette is about an hour (35 ninutes by Bart!) from downtown San Francisco. It's also about 30 minutes from Oakland Airport. And it's about 10 minutes from Walnut Creek, an East Bay hub. So you can really stay almost anywhere in the Bay Area. There are many options at just about every price point. It's even 45 minutes from Napa if you want to make it a thing!

We haven't done a ton of research, but there is a block of rooms at the Courtyard Marriot in Pleasant Hill. If you mention our wedding, you will get the group rate while supplies last.

The Marriott in Walnut Creek a .6 mile walk from the Walnut Creek Bart, also in downtown Walnut Creek with more shops/restaurants. Embassy Suites by Hilton in Walnut Creek is very close to Pleasant Hill Bart stop, only .2 miles. Residence Inn by Marriott in Walnut Creek is a .4 mile walk from Walnut Creek Bart stop. Lafayette Park Hotel (fanciest option listed here) is a 1.5 mile walk to Lafayette Bart station.
