When is the RSVP deadline?

May 31, 2020. You can RSVP here!

Where is it?

The wedding ceremony will be in Jack's parents' backyard. Their address is: 22 Reliez Valley Court, Lafayette, CA 94549. The wedding reception is a 7 minute drive away at The Pleasant Hill Community Center (Perera Pavilion 320 Civic Dr., Pleasant Hill, CA).

Because parking is limited at the Moxons', we will be running buses from Lafayette BART to Jack's parents' house for the ceremony and then from the ceremony back to the reception.

Please let us know in your RSVP if you'll be driving or taking BART (please take BART!).

Where should I stay?

Lafayette is about an hour (35 minutes by BART!) from downtown San Francisco. It's also about 30 minutes from Oakland Airport and about 50 minutes from SFO. And it's about 10 minutes from Walnut Creek, an East Bay hub. So you can really stay almost anywhere in the Bay Area. There are many options at just about every price point. It's even 45 minutes from Napa if you want to make it a thing!

We haven't done a ton of research, but there is a block of rooms at the Courtyard Marriott in Pleasant Hill. If you mention our wedding, you will get the group rate while supplies last.

Other hotels with prices as reported in early February (may not include taxes or parking):

Marriott in Walnut Creek – $154-174 0.6 mile walk from the Walnut Creek BART, and in downtown Walnut Creek with shops/restaurants

Embassy Suites by Hilton in Walnut Creek – $156-174 0.2 mile walk from Pleasant Hill BART

Residence Inn by Marriott in Walnut Creek – $209+ 0.4 mile walk from Walnut Creek BART

Lafayette Park Hotel - $314-370 1.5 mile walk to Lafayette BART

Are kids welcome?

Please leave your children at home so you can enjoy a night out kiddos-free!

What time should I arrive?

The wedding ceremony starts at 4:10pm. Our goal is to have everyone seated in the backyard at 4pm so please arrive at the Moxons' by 4pm. If you are taking the bus from Lafayette BART please arrive by 3:35pm for a 3:45pm departure.

Where should I park?

If you are driving to the ceremony, please park on Reliez Valley Court. We ask that you do your best to carpool or better yet take BART to Lafayette Station where a bus will be available to take you to the ceremony and the reception.

There is ample parking at the reception site (Perera Pavilion).

Will food be served?

Yes! We will have appetizers upon arrival at the reception, followed by a taco buffet with plenty of vegan-friendly and non-vegan options!
