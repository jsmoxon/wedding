When is the RSVP deadline?

June 15, 2021. Please RSVP via your Paperless Post invitation!

Where is it?

The wedding ceremony and reception will take place at The Pleasant Hill Community Center (Perera Pavilion 320 Civic Dr., Pleasant Hill, CA).

What about SARS-COV-2?

As the vaccine is more widely distributed we expect to continue to see Covid rates fall. We will of course comply with local regulations and guidelines to make sure that the wedding is safe and fun. As of now, we expect all of our guests to have been vaccinated long before August August.

Where should I stay?

Depending on traffic, Pleasant Hill is about 45-60 minutes from San Francisco, 30-40 minutes from Oakland/Berkeley, and about 15 minutes from Walnut Creek. There are many options at just about every price point. It's even 45 minutes from Napa if you're looking to make it a long weekend!

Here are a few ideas for hotels near the venue:

1. Residence Inn Pleasant Hill Concord: .8 mile walk from the wedding venue.

2. Walnut Creek Marriott: 5 miles to venue

3. Residence Inn Walnut Creek: 5.6 miles to venue

4. Holiday Inn Express Walnut Creek: 4.3 miles to venue

5. Embassy Suites Hilton Walnut Creek: 3.6 miles to venue

6. Hyatt House Pleasant Hill: 2.2 miles to venue

7. Lafayette Park Hotel & Spa: 6 miles to venue

Should I bring my kids?

Please leave your children at home so you can enjoy a night out kiddos-free!

Where should I park?

There is ample parking at the reception site.

Will food be served?

Yes! We will have appetizers after the ceremony, followed by a taco buffet with plenty of vegan-friendly, gluten-free and non-vegan options!

What should I wear?

Cocktail Attire - We're excited to dress up after a year in sweatpants! And make sure you bring your dancing shoes!

Are the ceremony and reception locations wheelchair accessible?

Yes.
