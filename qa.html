When is the RSVP deadline?

June 15, 2021. Please RSVP via Paperless Post!

Where is it?

The wedding ceremony and reception will take place at The Pleasant Hill Community Center (Perera Pavilion 320 Civic Dr., Pleasant Hill, CA).

Where should I stay?

Depending on traffic, Pleasant Hill is about 45-60 minutes from San Francisco and about 30-40 minutes from Oakland/Berkeley. It's about 10 minutes from Walnut Creek, an East Bay hub. So you can really stay almost anywhere in the Bay Area. There are many options at just about every price point. It's even 45 minutes from Napa if you want to make it a thing!

We haven't done a ton of research, but here are a few ideas:

Other hotels with prices as reported in early February (may not include taxes or parking):

Marriott in Walnut Creek – $154-174 0.6 mile walk from the Walnut Creek BART, and in downtown Walnut Creek with shops/restaurants

Embassy Suites by Hilton in Walnut Creek – $156-174 0.2 mile walk from Pleasant Hill BART

Residence Inn by Marriott in Walnut Creek – $209+ 0.4 mile walk from Walnut Creek BART

Lafayette Park Hotel - $314-370 1.5 mile walk to Lafayette BART

Are kids invited?

Please leave your children at home so you can enjoy a night out kiddos-free!

Please arrive before 4:30 as we will be starting the ceremony then!
